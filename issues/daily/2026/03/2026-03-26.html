Every frontier model scores under 1% on ARC-AGI-3 as OpenAI crosses $120B and Google ships 6x memory compression

The ARC-AGI-3 benchmark placed every frontier model — from Google Gemini Pro to OpenAI GPT-5.4 High to Anthropic Claude Opus — below 1% of human performance on interactive reasoning, while OpenAI's funding round expanded to $120 billion at a $730 billion valuation, Google Research published TurboQuant delivering 6x KV-cache compression with zero accuracy loss, and the Pentagon formalized Palantir's Maven AI as a core military system with a $13.4 billion FY2026 budget.

What matters today

ARC-AGI-3 BENCHMARK

ARC-AGI-3 placed all frontier LLMs below 1% of human performance on interactive, goal-free reasoning environments — the benchmark's first major format change since 2019.

  • All frontier LLMs scored below 1%; best AI agent (StochasticGoose) reached 12.58% vs 100% human baseline.
  • Google Gemini Pro: 0.37% · OpenAI GPT-5.4 High: 0.26% · Anthropic Claude Opus: 0.25%.
  • Format change from static grid puzzles to turn-based game environments with no stated goals — success requires learning rules through interaction.
  • $2M+ prize pool across three tracks; winning solutions must be open-sourced.
  • Benchmark ships as MIT-licensed, pip-installable Python toolkit; benchmark is unsaturated.

OpenAI's CFO confirmed an additional $10 billion close, pushing the total round past $120 billion at a $730 billion pre-money valuation.

  • $120B+ total round at $730B pre-money valuation — surpassed initial $100B target.

  • Proceeds target compute infrastructure and product expansion.
  • Microsoft's participation continues alongside its primary cloud and Copilot infrastructure partnership with OpenAI.

GOOGLE TURBOQUANT

Google Research published TurboQuant, a two-stage algorithm delivering 6x KV-cache memory compression at 3-bit quantization with no measurable accuracy degradation and no retraining required.

  • 6x KV-cache memory reduction at 3-bit quantization — zero accuracy loss across question answering, code generation, and summarization benchmarks.
  • 8x speedup in attention logit computation on Nvidia H100s vs 32-bit baseline using 4-bit TurboQuant.
  • Algorithm combines PolarQuant (Cartesian-to-polar conversion) and QJL (Johnson-Lindenstrauss Transform for key compression).
  • No model retraining or fine-tuning required — drop-in for existing deployments.
  • Code and papers freely available including for enterprise use with no licensing restrictions. TechCrunch coverage.

PENTAGON MAVEN AI

The Pentagon designated Palantir's Maven Smart System as an official program of record, locking in multi-year funding with a $13.4 billion FY2026 AI budget line — up from $480 million in May 2024.

  • $480M (2024) → $13.4B FY2026 — 28x budget growth in under two years.
  • 20,000+ active military users; 1,000 targets processed in 24 hours during Operation Epic Fury.
  • $13.4B covers unmanned systems ($9.4B), autonomy ($1.6B), maritime ($1.7B), software ($1.2B).
  • Oversight transfers from the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency to the Pentagon's Chief Digital AI Office.
  • Program of record status locks in funding continuity across budget cycles; designation directed by Deputy Secretary Steve Feinberg to complete before September 2026.

  • $25,000 for first universal jailbreak clearing all ten bio/chem questions in bio bounty track.

  • Private campaigns run periodically targeting specific harm types.

Research worth watching

  • EVA: Efficient Reinforcement Learning for End-to-End Video Agent

    RL framework for video understanding agents using Kahneman-Tversky Optimization and Generalized Reward Policy Optimization. Iterative planning with adaptive reasoning across multiple video benchmarks.

  • T-MAP: Red-Teaming LLM Agents with Trajectory-aware Evolutionary Search

    Evolutionary search for adversarial prompts that bypass safety in tool-using agents via execution trajectory vulnerabilities. Directly applicable to agentic product security evaluation.

  • UI-Voyager: A Self-Evolving GUI Agent Learning via Failed Experience

    Mobile GUI agent using rejection fine-tuning and group relative self-distillation. Self-improves from failed automation attempts using dense step-level supervision.

  • Why Does Self-Distillation (Sometimes) Degrade LLM Reasoning?

    Self-distillation suppresses uncertainty expression and degrades math reasoning on out-of-distribution tasks. Epistemic verbalization identified as critical to preserving reasoning during model compression.
